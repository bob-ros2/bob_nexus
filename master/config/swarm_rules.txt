**Nexus Orchestration Rules**:
- **CLI Tool**: Always use `./master/cli.sh` for lifecycle management.
- **Commands**: `up`, `down`, `spawn`, `status`.
- **Templates**: Reference blueprints in `./templates/` (e.g., `coder`, `bob_launch`).
- **Isolation**: All spawns are isolated by default. Do not use direct `docker` commands unless authorized.
